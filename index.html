About

Greetings from Alumni Association IIT Kanpur & Entrepreneurship Cell IIT Kanpur! We bring to you, IITK Combinator. An ecosystem to bring together students, professors and alumni of IIT Kanpur from different parts of the startup journey where they can help each other grow. We want to onboard startups, mentors, promoters and investors to connect them with each other and foster entrepreneurship. We will conduct an online event every month to connect the shortlisted startups with mentors & investors. Interested individuals can fill the relevant form below.

Get the expert’s advice

Get connected with IIT Kanpur Alumni who have immeasurable experience and have been in the startup space for years. They are known to have combated multiple battles and their experience will rightfully guide you in having a smooth startup journey of your own. With their mentorship your startup can achieve great heights.

Expand your business with great connections

Through our Founder groups, get help in expanding your business by connecting with founders working in the same domain. Founder groups will help you use other IITK Founders’ experiences and contacts to help you grow. Furthermore, have a chance to grab mentorship and investment opportunities in our networking events.

Mentor / Invest in great Startups

If you are an investor looking for a good opportunity to support, who better than the brightest minds of the country. Find great startups to mentor and invest in, founded by IIT Kanpur Alumni with great potential and zeal to outperform. You can also join a proposed Angel Fund to invest in startups founded by IITK Students, Professors and Alumni.
